kheer

A lusciously creamy dessert with fragrant cardamom and nuts, a sweet treat enjoyed on festive occasions.

Ingredients:
  1. Rice
  2. milk
  3. sugar
  4. cardamom
  5. saffron
  6. nuts(almond, pista, etc.)
  7. raisins
Steps:
  1. Cook rice in milk until soft and creamy.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom, saffron, and nuts.
  3. Garnish with raisins before serving.
