Masala Dosa

A crispy, thin crepe filled with a spicy potato filling, served with chutneys and sambar, a perfect combination of flavors and textures.

Ingredients:
  1. Rice
  2. urad dal (black lentils)
  3. fenugreek seeds
  4. potatoes
  5. mustard seeds
  6. curry leaves
  7. turmeric
  8. green chilies
  9. oil
  10. salt
Steps:
  1. Soak rice, dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 6 hours.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter, ferment for 8 hours.
  3. Prepare a spiced potato filling.
  4. Spread dosa batter on a hot griddle, add filling, fold, and serve with chutneys and sambar.
