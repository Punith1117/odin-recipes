Vangi Bath

Vangi Bath is a delightful rice dish bursting with the flavors of eggplant, spices, and masalas, making it a satisfying and aromatic meal.

Ingredients:
  1. 1 cup rice
  2. 1 small eggplant (brinjal)
  3. 1 onion, finely chopped
  4. 1 tomato, chopped
  5. 2-3 green chilies, slit
  6. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  7. 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  8. A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  9. 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  10. 1 tsp vangi bath masala powder
  11. 2 tbsp peanuts
  12. Few curry leaves
  13. 2 tbsp oil
  14. 2 tbsp oil
  15. Salt to taste
  16. Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
Steps:
  1. Cook rice and spread it on a plate to cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and peanuts. Roast until peanuts are golden.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chilies, onions, and saut√© until onions are translucent.
  4. Add eggplant, turmeric, salt, and cook till eggplant is soft.
  5. Add tomatoes, vangi bath masala powder, and cook until tomatoes are mushy.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix well with the masala.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
